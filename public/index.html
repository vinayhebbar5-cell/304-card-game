How to Play & Rules

Objective

Team up with your partner (opposite seat) to win tricks and achieve your bidding target, or hold the opponents below theirs. The first team to reach +5 Golden Points wins the match!

Card Rankings & Points

Bidding Targets

Players bid counter-clockwise based on their first 3 cards. The bidder's team must hold the opponents below the target limit:

Special Mechanics

  • Secret Trump: The bid winner secretly picks the Trump card from their hand. It is revealed on Trick 1.
  • Marriages: King & Queen of the same suit. Shown after winning your team's first trick. Trump marriage adds +4 pts; other marriage adds +2 pts (directly subtracted from opponent's score). Playing either K or Q beforehand breaks the marriage.
  • Vakkai (Solo Contract): Overrides all bids. Caller must win all 6 tricks alone. If the partner wins a trick or plays a higher card (Partner Sabotage), the contract fails. Wins or loses 3 Golden Points.
  • Kotu: If one team sweeps the first 5 tricks, the Trick 6 leader can call Kotu. Sweeping the final trick adds +2 Golden Points (or subtracts 2 from opponents). Failure penalizes you -1 Golden Point.
